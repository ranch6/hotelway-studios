Questions

The practical details.

Will a shoot disrupt our guests?

No. We shoot with a small footprint — one person, no crew sprawl, no roped-off spaces. Occupied areas are shot in quiet windows agreed with your team, and guest privacy is absolute: no faces, ever.

How quickly are assets delivered?

Films and stills are delivered within two weeks of the shoot day, organized by placement and named so your team can find everything without a walkthrough call.

Who owns the content?

You receive a plain-language license covering organic use across your channels; paid-media usage is available as an add-on. The studio retains portfolio rights. No surprises buried in fine print.

Why faceless content?

No talent releases, no influencer risk, nothing that ages out when a creator's image changes. First-person framing also converts better for hospitality — the viewer places themselves in the room instead of watching someone else enjoy it.

Do you travel?

Based in New York, available worldwide. Travel is billed at cost, and for destination properties we can often pair your shoot with others in the region to share those costs.

About

Hotelway Studios is a New York–based creative studio focused on one thing: how a stay feels from the guest's side of the door. We make faceless, POV-driven photo and video for hotels, boutique properties, and considered short-term stays.
